Case Study: Enterprise Operational Insights & Automation Suite

A look at a solution designed specifically for US Dental Depot to manage liability, eliminate penalties, and drive proactive management.

The Challenge

  • Hidden Liability: Critical due dates buried in static spreadsheets.
  • Reactive Firefighting: Bills known to be late only after the penalty is applied.
  • Manual Fatigue: Hours wasted manually checking statuses instead of focusing on strategy.

The AMIE Solution

  • Operational Insights Dashboard: Real-time liability breakdown, overdue amounts, total penalty, and cash flow requirements.
  • Automated Daily Liability Bot: Runs 24/7 via Google Apps Script, sending a prioritized, actionable email (Critical, Action Needed, Due Today).
  • Tech Advantage: Built entirely on the native Google Workspace (Sheets, Apps Script, Looker Studio).

Value & Financial ROI

  • Eliminate Penalties: Current annual penalties estimated at over $12,000+.
  • Total Investment: $9,000 (One-time)
  • Recurring Software Costs: $0 (Native Google Workspace).
  • Payback Period: Less than 9 Months. (Solution is self-funding.)

The dashboard provides instant visualization of financial risk, moving data from static spreadsheets to actionable intelligence.
